Biography

I am now a senior research fellow at the Department of Computer Science, University College London, working with Prof. Danail Stoyanov in WEISS. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2019, supervised by Prof. Pheng-Ann Heng and Prof. Chi-Wing Fu.

My research interests include artificial intelligence and its applications on medical image computing, robotic surgical data science. I recently work on spatial-temporal representation learning, data efficient learning, and multi-modality learning.

SRV group, WEISS is looking for self-motivated summer interns (2022), to work on the research areas of robotic surgery intelligence and surgical 3D vision. Welcome to drop me an email with your CV. Remote collaboration is also welcome.
